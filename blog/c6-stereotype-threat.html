Stereotype Threat

It can effect anyone!

26th April, 2015

Have you ever been in a situtation where you are expected to do great or bad in a examination?

Being from an Indian family, I was always expected to do really well at everything that had to with math but not so well in english or reading. Becasue of this, when I was taking examinations I would pay more attention to the math sections resulting in higher scores compared to my english section.

This could be reason why I never liked english as a subject in school or college. Regardless of how I felt about any subject I was always expected get perfect scores in Math portions of all examination. This way of thinking forced me to only consider careers that used math.

This stereotype helps to motivate me to do really well in all math related classes to a point where I stated enjoying math. This would be a positive side of Stereotypeing.
