Creating my own Website

An Interesting Experience!

17th May, 2015

Having the opportunity to create my own website was an excellent experience. Even though my website is lacking content and special effects, I liked I was given the opportunity to lay a foundation that I could build on in the future.

I personally had never written blogs before DBC or ever thought I would. I have started to realize that blogging is a great way to share your experience and a way to see if you truly understand a topic. Before blogging there were times when I though I had understood a topics like Recursion, Classes, and Hash tables but while trying to write about it I found out that even-though I knew the topic I had a hard time explaining it in words.

I hope to continue to work on my website and improve upon it as I learn new concepts and technologies.
